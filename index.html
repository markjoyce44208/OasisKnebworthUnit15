About the Documentary



The Knebworth 1996 documentary documents the run up, days of and lasting cultural impact of one of the largest concerts in the UK - 500'000 people attended across a two day period.

Featuring stories about the dedicated fans who would stop at nothing to see the band at the concert of a lifetime, and anecdotes from band members themselves.

Includes footage that has never been broadcast before, such as high quality clips of the concert itself, as well as interviews with fans the days of the concert.

The blu-ray disc includes the full two nights at Knebworth in remastered high defintion quality - the second night was never released in broadcast quality.
